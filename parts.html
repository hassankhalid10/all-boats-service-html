The OEM Advantage: Quality You Can Trust

At All Boats Service Center, we believe that what goes into your boat is just as important as who installs it. We exclusively stock and recommend Original Equipment Manufacturer (OEM) parts. Unlike generic aftermarket components, OEM parts are:

  • Precision Engineered: Designed specifically for your engine's unique tolerances.
  • Warranty-Safe: Using genuine parts ensures you stay within your manufacturer's warranty guidelines.
  • Built for the Marine Environment: Higher resistance to corrosion and stress than "marine-grade" imitations.

Parts for Every Project

Our parts department caters to both professional mechanics and DIY owners. Our inventory includes:

  • Maintenance Essentials: Filters, impellers, spark plugs, and specialized marine lubricants.
  • Electrical & Fuel: Starters, alternators, fuel pumps, and EFI components.
  • Drive & Propeller: Propellers (aluminum and stainless), bellows kits, and gimbal bearings.
  • Accessories: Bilge pumps, lighting, and marine batteries.
