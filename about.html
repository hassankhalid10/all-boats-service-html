Our Story: From the Dock to the Dealership

Every great boat story starts with a passion for the water. Ours began right here in the Lake Cumberland region. Founded on the belief that boaters deserved a higher standard of technical expertise combined with hometown honesty, All Boats Service Center grew from a modest repair shop into the area's premier #1 Parts and Service Dealer.

We didn't get here by accident. We built our reputation one engine, one trailer, and one customer at a time. Over the years, we've seen the industry change—from carburetors to complex EFI systems—but our core mission has remained the same: Keeping families on the water and making memories.

Our Culture: Built by Boaters, for Boaters

At All Boats, we don't just hire mechanics; we hire enthusiasts. Our company culture is defined by:

  • The Pursuit of Mastery: We invest heavily in our team, ensuring our technicians receive ongoing factory training from Mercury, Volvo Penta, and Yamaha.
  • Integrity Above All: We believe in "Fixed Right the First Time." We provide transparent estimates and honest advice, treating your boat as if it were our own.
  • The "Lake Life" Mentality: We know that a weekend lost to a mechanical failure isn't just an inconvenience—it's a missed opportunity for family time. That urgency drives everything we do.

Rooted in the Lake Cumberland Community

We aren't just a business on the side of the road; we are a part of the fabric of Bronston and Burnside. Our commitment to the community goes beyond the service bays.

  • Supporting Local Events: From sponsoring local fishing tournaments to participating in Lake Cumberland clean-up initiatives, we believe in preserving the waters we love.
  • Economic Impact: As a local employer, we are proud to provide high-skill jobs to the hardworking people of Pulaski and Wayne counties.
  • A Resource for Boaters: We strive to be a hub of information for local and visiting boaters alike, offering advice on lake safety, navigation, and local conditions.
