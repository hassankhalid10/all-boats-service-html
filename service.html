Our Core Marine Services

Whether you're looking for a seasonal tune-up or a major engine overhaul, our factory-trained technicians utilize the latest diagnostic technology to ensure your vessel is lake-ready.

Expert Mechanical & Engine Repair

As an authorized service dealer for the industry's top manufacturers, we provide specialized care that keeps your warranty intact and your engine performing at its peak.

  • Authorized Brands: Mercury, MerCruiser, Volvo Penta, Yamaha, Suzuki, and OMC.
  • Computer Diagnostics: Precision troubleshooting for EFI and DFI systems.
  • Major Rebuilds: Complete engine and drive-train overhauls.

Routine Maintenance & Tune-Ups

Preventative care is the key to a stress-free boating season. Our team follows manufacturer-specific checklists to catch minor issues before they become major headaches.

  • Oil & Filter Services: Using only high-grade marine lubricants.
  • Water Pump & Impeller Service: Critical cooling system maintenance.
  • Fuel System Service: Fuel stabilization and filter replacement for maximum efficiency.

Winterization & Spring Commissioning

Don't let a Kentucky winter damage your craft. Our comprehensive winterization package ensures your boat is protected from freezing temperatures and corrosion.

  • Engine Prep: Antifreeze flush and fogging.
  • System Protection: Freshwater system draining and battery maintenance.
  • Summer Prep: Full "de-winterization" and safety inspections every spring.
