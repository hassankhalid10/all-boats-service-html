Our Core Marine Services

Whatever repair or tune-up you need, our service center provides factory-certified marine technicians and shop equipment to keep your boat running its best, on every body of water.

Expert Mechanical & Engine Repair

  • Computer Diagnostics: Identifying complex performance issues with manufacturer-grade tools.
  • Major Rebuilds: Complete restoration and repowering of inboard and outboard engines.
  • Lower Unit Service: Gear lube, seal, and bearing inspection and replacement.
Request Service Learn More

Routine Maintenance & Tune-Ups

Preventative care is key to a successful boating season. Our team follows manufacturer-specified schedules to ensure your boat is always ready for the water.

  • Oil & Filter Changes: Using top-grade marine lubricants.
  • Spark Plug & Ignition Service: Ensuring reliable starts and efficient combustion.
  • Fuel System Cleaning: Removing carbon and ethanol residue to optimize fuel efficiency.
Request Service Learn More

Winterization & Spring Commissioning

Don't risk costly damage from freezing temperatures—let us prep your vessel for the off-season. When spring arrives, we'll have it back on the water without a hitch.

  • Winterization: Fuel stabilization, antifreeze flush, battery storage, engine fogging.
  • Spring Commissioning: Battery installation, fluid checks, system diagnostics, water testing.
Request Service Learn More

Professional Trailer Service

Keep your trailer road-ready and safe with our complete trailer maintenance services.

  • Bearings, brakes, and bunk inspections
  • Light, wiring, and coupler diagnostics
  • Tire replacement and full alignment
